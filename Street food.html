Street Feast – Indian Street Food Menu 🍢

North Indian Delights

  • Pani Puri / Golgappa – Crispy hollow puris filled with tangy tamarind water and spiced mashed potatoes.
  • Aloo Tikki Chaat – Spiced potato patties topped with yogurt, chutneys, and sev.
  • Chole Bhature – Fluffy fried bread with spicy chickpea curry.
  • Pav Bhaji – Buttery mashed vegetable curry served with toasted buns.
  • Raj Kachori – Giant crisp puri stuffed with yogurt, chutneys, and crunchy fillings.
  • Samosa – Golden pastry stuffed with spiced potatoes and peas.
  • Bread Pakora – Bread slices dipped in gram flour batter and fried.
  • Chole Kulche – Soft kulcha bread with spicy chickpeas.
  • Matar Kulcha – Peas curry served with soft kulcha bread.
  • Bedmi Puri with Aloo Sabzi – Spiced wheat puris with potato curry.

West Indian Favourites

  • Vada Pav – Spicy potato fritter in a soft bun with chutneys.
  • Bhel Puri – Puffed rice tossed with veggies, chutneys, and spices.
  • Dabeli – Sweet and spicy mashed potato stuffed bun with pomegranate.
  • Sev Puri – Flat puris topped with potatoes, chutneys, and sev.
  • Misal Pav – Sprouted lentil curry topped with farsan, served with bread.
  • Kanda Bhajiya – Crispy onion fritters.
  • Bombay Sandwich – Layered vegetable sandwich with chutneys.
  • Frankie Roll – Spiced filling wrapped in a soft roti.
  • Kothimbir Vadi – Steamed and fried coriander-spiced snack.
  • Bhakarwadi – Sweet and spicy spiral snacks.

East Indian Specials

  • Momos – Steamed dumplings with vegetable or meat filling.
  • Egg Roll / Chicken Roll – Flaky paratha stuffed with egg or chicken.
  • Ghugni Chaat – Spiced yellow peas curry topped with onions and chutneys.
  • Puchka – Bengali-style tangy pani puri.
  • Jhalmuri – Spicy puffed rice mix with mustard oil and peanuts.
  • Singara – Bengali samosa with potato and cauliflower filling.
  • Chowmein – Indian-style stir-fried noodles with vegetables.
  • Mughlai Paratha – Egg-stuffed flaky paratha.
  • Fish Fry – Crispy fried marinated fish.
  • Telebhaja – Assorted vegetable fritters.

South Indian Flavours

  • Masala Dosa – Crisp dosa filled with spiced potato masala.
  • Idli with Chutney – Soft steamed rice cakes with coconut chutney.
  • Medu Vada – Crispy lentil doughnuts served with sambar.
  • Uttapam – Thick dosa topped with onions, tomatoes, and chillies.
  • Pesarattu – Green gram dosa with ginger chutney.
  • Bhajji – Deep-fried onion, chilli, or plantain fritters.
  • Bonda – Fried potato dumplings in gram flour batter.
  • Kothu Parotta – Shredded parotta stir-fried with spices.
  • Sundal – Spiced chickpeas tempered with curry leaves.
  • Punugulu – Crispy dosa batter fritters.
